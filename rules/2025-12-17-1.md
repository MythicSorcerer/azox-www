# Azox Network Server Rules

Welcome to the Azox Network! To ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for all players, please ACTUALLY read and follow these rules carefully. Violations may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the server.

---

## 1. General Conduct

1. Treat all players with respect and courtesy
2. No harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind
3. Keep chat appropriate and family-friendly
4. No excessive profanity or offensive language
5. Don't do anything you wouldn't do in person
6. Keep in mind that this isn't 2b2t

## 2. Cheating and Exploiting

1. Absolutely no hacking, cheating, or use of unauthorized modifications. This includes misuse of operator privileges and hack clients.
2. No exploiting game bugs or glitches for personal advantage
3. No duplication of items or currency
4. Report any discovered exploits to staff immediately

## 3. Fair Play

1. No griefing or intentional destruction of other players' builds
2. Respect faction territories and boundaries
3. No spawn camping or excessive targeting of new players
4. Play within the spirit of the game

## 4. Permitted Modifications

1. Minimap, freecam, and fullbright mods are permitted due to their nature and difficulty to enforce
2. These give minimal advantage over existing locator features
3. This policy may be reconsidered at a later time

---

## 5. PvP Rules

1. PvP is always enabled - be prepared for combat
2. No retreat once combat has begun in designated areas

## 6. Prohibited PvP Modifications *(Severe Violation)*

1. Any and all modifications that modify client behavior are disallowed
2. Prohibited mods include but are not limited to:
   - Aimbot
   - Criticals
   - Killaura
   - Killauralegit (can be detected; server's not stupid, fyi)
   - Anti-KB
   - Anti-Slowdown (i.e., cobwebs, water)
   - Anti-cactus
   - Anti-hunger
   - Other mods that give PvP advantages

---

## 7. Trading Standards

1. No scamming or fraudulent trades
2. Honor all agreed-upon deals and contracts
3. No market manipulation through artificial scarcity
4. Report suspicious trading activity to staff

## 8. Resource Management

1. No hoarding of essential resources to grief other players
2. Respect mining claims and established territories
3. No stealing from faction storage without permission
4. Share community resources fairly

---

## 9. Chat Rules

1. English only in global chat
2. Use appropriate channels for different topics
3. No spamming, advertising, or excessive caps
4. No sharing personal information or doxxing
5. Please keep NSFW content to a minimum in chat

## 10. Discord Integration

1. Follow Discord server rules when linked accounts are active
2. No ban evasion through alternate accounts (We track IPs)
3. Maintain consistent identity across platforms

---

## 11. Warning System

1. **Light Penalty:** Some minor one-offs may be simply resolved with an explanation of rules.
2. **First Offense:** Warning and explanation of rules, possible ban (0-24h)
3. **Second Offense:** Temporary ban (24-48 hours)
4. **Third Offense:** Extended ban (1-7 days)
5. **Severe Violations:** Immediate permanent ban

## 12. Appeal Process

1. Appeals can be submitted through Discord or website (register and DM an admin)
2. Include evidence and explanation of circumstances
3. Staff decisions are final after review process
4. False appeals may result in extended penalties

---

## 13. Staff Interaction

1. Respect staff decisions and authority
2. Report rule violations through proper channels
3. No arguing with staff in public chat
4. Use ticket system for disputes or appeals

## 14. Staff Conduct

1. Staff members are held to higher standards
2. No abuse of powers or favoritism
3. Transparent decision-making process
4. Regular review of staff actions

---

## 15. Community Events

1. Participation is voluntary but encouraged
2. Follow event-specific guidelines
3. Respect event organizers and participants
4. Have fun and maintain sportsmanship

---

**Remember:** These rules are subject to change. Players are responsible for staying updated on current rules. Ignorance of rules is not an excuse for violations.

**Questions?** Contact staff through Discord or ask in game.
