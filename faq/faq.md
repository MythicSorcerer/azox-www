## üéÆ Getting Started

### How do I join the Azox Network server?

To join Azox Network:

1. Open Minecraft Java Edition (version 1.20.1 or later)
2. Click "Multiplayer" from the main menu
3. Click "Add Server" or "Direct Connect"
4. Enter the server IP: **play.azox.network**
5. Click "Join Server" and start playing!

For detailed instructions, visit our [Play Now](/play-now/) page.

### What Minecraft version do I need?

Azox Network supports Minecraft Java Edition versions **1.20.1 to 1.21.3**. We recommend using the latest version for the best experience and access to all features.

Bedrock Edition is not currently supported, but we're working on cross-platform compatibility for future updates.

### Do I need to register an account?

No registration is required to play on the server! Simply join with your Minecraft account and start playing immediately.

However, registering on our website gives you access to:

- Forum discussions and community features
- Real-time messaging system
- Event notifications and updates
- Player statistics and achievements

## üè∞ Gameplay & Features

### What game modes are available?

Azox Network offers multiple exciting game modes:

- **Survival:** Classic survival experience with enhanced features
- **Creative:** Build without limits in our creative world
- **PvP Arena:** Competitive player vs player combat
- **Mini-Games:** Various fun mini-games and challenges
- **Events:** Special seasonal events and tournaments

Use `/server [mode]` to switch between different game modes.

### How do I claim land and protect my builds?

Land protection is easy with our claiming system:

1. Use `/claim` to claim the area you're standing in
2. Use `/trust [player]` to allow friends to build in your claim
3. Use `/untrust [player]` to remove building permissions
4. Use `/abandonclaim` to remove a claim you no longer need

New players get 100 claim blocks to start, with more earned through playtime. Visit our [Claim Calculator](/tools/claim/) to plan your builds!

### What are the server rules?

Our core rules ensure a fun experience for everyone:

- No griefing or destroying other players' builds
- Be respectful in chat - no harassment or toxic behavior
- No cheating, hacking, or exploiting bugs
- Keep builds appropriate for all ages
- Follow staff instructions and respect their decisions

For complete rules and guidelines, visit our [Rules](/rules/) page.

## üí∞ Economy & Trading

### How does the server economy work?

Azox Network features a balanced player-driven economy:

- **Currency:** Azox Coins (AC) - earned through gameplay
- **Jobs:** Choose from various jobs like mining, farming, building
- **Shops:** Create your own shop or trade at spawn market
- **Auctions:** Bid on rare items in server-wide auctions

Use `/balance` to check your coins and `/jobs` to browse available jobs.

### How do I set up a shop?

Setting up a shop is simple:

1. Place a chest with items you want to sell
2. Hold the item and type `/shop create [price]`
3. Right-click the chest to link it to your shop
4. Players can right-click to buy from your shop

You can also rent a shop plot at spawn market for high-traffic sales!

## üõ†Ô∏è Technical Support

### I'm having connection issues. What should I do?

If you're having trouble connecting:

1. Check that you're using the correct IP: **play.azox.network**
2. Ensure you're using a supported Minecraft version (1.20.1-1.21.3)
3. Try restarting your Minecraft client
4. Check your internet connection and firewall settings
5. If issues persist, contact us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/azox)

You can also check our server status on the homepage for any ongoing maintenance.

### My items disappeared or I lost progress. Can you help?

We understand how frustrating item loss can be. Here's what to do:

1. Check if you died - items may be at your death location
2. Use `/back` to return to your last location
3. Contact staff immediately with details about what happened
4. Provide screenshots or evidence if possible

Our staff can review server logs and restore items in cases of server errors or bugs. Player mistakes (like falling in lava) generally cannot be restored.

### How do I report a bug or cheater?

To report issues:

- **In-game:** Use `/report [player] [reason]` for rule violations
- **Discord:** Contact staff in our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/azox)
- **Forum:** Post in our [community forum](/forum/)
- **Email:** Send details to support@azox.network

Include as much detail as possible, including player names, timestamps, and screenshots.
